About me

I was born (in 1993), raised and currently live in the south of the Netherlands, near the city of Eindhoven. At the nearby university I first studied computer science and later educational science. I am a professional software engineer. My personal mission is to bring quality programming education to the world.

I am very interested in uncovering and deeply understanding fundamentals; of programming, of education, of life in general. This allows me to act with purpose and remove friction. To me, it results in a happier life.

A great way to develop a deep understanding is by collecting many perspectives. I do this by reading a lot, watching a lot and striking up conversations with people face to face or on the internet.

I enjoy living a simple and balanced life through minimalism and active personal development.

More concretely, I love fantasy literature, submission grappling, peace and quiet.
