About me

I was born (in 1993), raised and currently live in the south of the Netherlands, near the city of Eindhoven. At the university there I first studied computer science and later educational science. I am a software engineer. My personal mission is to bring quality programming education to the world.

I crave a deep understanding of fundamentals; of programming, of education, of life itself. Perhaps out of laziness (read: efficiency). I find that a strong grasp of fundamentals makes all the rest so much easier.

To reach the core, one must dig deep. Luckily, I find the digging very enjoyable. Collecting many perspectives by reading, watching and conversating with an open mind. Mulling, discussing, reflecting, experimenting. With each iteration the constellations appear a little clearer.

I enjoy living a simple, thoughtful and balanced life. I love fantasy literature, submission grappling, peace and quiet.

About this website

I have read many blogs and by doing so collected many perspectives and inspiring thoughts. With this website I hope to likewise contribute.

Also, I view a blog as casting out a fishing rod. Hopefully at some point somebody will bite and share their comments with me. Feedback and discussion are great.

For a while I had been entertaining the thought of starting a blog. What pushed me over the edge was a thread on a forum where I ended up sharing some thoughts on my mission. Somebody asked if I had a blog. Now I do.
